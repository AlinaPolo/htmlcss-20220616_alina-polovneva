Saint-Petersburg Part 1

Saint-Petersburg is the second largest city in Russia and one of the most beautiful cities in the world. The city is called Northern Venice too.

The population of the city grew fast. Nowadays, it is near six million people.

Saint-Petersburg Part 2

There are lots of museums in the city. The Hermitage,for example, contains the richest collection of pictures and pieces of applied art in the world.

Saint-Petersburg Part 3

Saint Petersburg is also famous for its legendary white nights. Different festivals take place at this time.
